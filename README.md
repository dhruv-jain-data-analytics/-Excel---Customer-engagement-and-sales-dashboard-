# Customer Engagement & Sales Dashboard (Excel)

## Project Overview

This Excel dashboard was developed to analyze customer engagement, sales performance, order trends, and business growth for ABC Ltd. The dashboard provides management with a consolidated view of key business metrics and actionable insights for decision-making.

## Business Objective

* Track sales and order performance across multiple years.
* Analyze customer interactions and engagement levels.
* Monitor conversion rates and business growth.
* Identify top-performing and underperforming products, customers, and salespersons.
* Generate insights to support strategic business decisions.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales: ₹48.18 Cr+
* Total Orders: 448,732
* Average Order Value: ₹5.99 Lakh+
* Conversion Rate: Improved from 94.77% (2021) to 97.28% (2024)

## Key Insights

### Sales Performance

* Business is experiencing strong year-on-year growth.
* High-value transactions are contributing significantly to overall revenue.

### Customer Analysis

* Revenue is concentrated among a limited number of customers.
* Customer diversification can reduce dependency risk.

### Product Analysis

* Products P2, P4, and P6 are top revenue contributors.
* Products P9 and P11 show lower sales performance and may require strategic review.

### Salesperson Performance

* Top performers: S3, S4, and S16.
* Underperformers: S5 and S7.
* Additional training and performance improvement initiatives may be beneficial.

### Order Trends

* Demand patterns remain relatively consistent across products.
* Strong conversion rates indicate an effective sales strategy.

## Business Recommendations

1. Focus on scaling high-performing products.
2. Improve performance of low-demand products through targeted strategies.
3. Train underperforming sales representatives.
4. Reduce revenue concentration by expanding the customer base.
5. Continue leveraging the current sales strategy to maintain strong conversion rates.
